What we cannot help with

  • Homework / dissertation translations. We're a tiny team. Please cite published scholarly translations for academic work.
  • Commercial reprint of full tales for free. We're happy to grant permission, but commercial reprint of more than 500 words requires a short licence. Email us.
  • Custom translation requests for paid clients. Not at this stage — we focus on our editorial line.
  • Identifying or appraising antique Chinese books. We translate; we don't authenticate.

Otherwise — yes, we want to hear from you. 山有狐，月正红。
